What is Stock Maarket

The stock market is a platform where people buy and sell ownership in companies, called shares. It connects investors with businesses and allows you to grow your wealth over time.

Stocks / Shares - Ownership in a company
Stock Exchange - NSE, BSE
Broker - Zerodha, Groww, Upstox
Orders - Buy, Sell, Market Order,LimitOrder


Understand who participates:
Retail Traders
Investors
Institutional Investors (FIIs / DIIs)
Brokers
Each group affects market movement differently.


Choose a style that fits your learning:
Intraday Trading - Buy & sell same day
Swing Trading - Hold for days/weeks
Long-Term Investing - Hold for years
Scalping - Quick trades (advanced)
Beginners should start with long-term investing or swing trading.


Before entering the market, learn these basics:

Market Cap - Large, Mid, Small-cap
